History of Sulphuric Acid

Sulphuric Acid was discovered by Jabir Ibn Hayyan in the 8th century and 9thcentury the physian and alchemist Ibn Zakarya AlRazi studied the properties of sulphuric acid and obtain the substance by dry diatillation of minerals including iron(II) sulphate heptahydrate FeSO4.7H2O and copper (II)Sulphate pentahydrates CUSO4

When these compounds are heated they were converted into iron oxide and copper oxide giving off water and slphur trioxide.
